- Added `ScmDeliveryAdapter.read_delivery_snapshot()` and `ReadDeliverySnapshotTool`.
- Registered tool metadata as category `delivery`, risk `read_only`, side effect `none`, no workspace requirement, and audit required.
- Read the current run's frozen `PipelineRunModel.delivery_channel_snapshot_ref` and matching `DeliveryChannelSnapshotModel`.

- Enforced trace/run consistency and rejected mismatches with `TOOL_INPUT_SCHEMA_INVALID`.
- Returned `DELIVERY_SNAPSHOT_MISSING` for missing snapshots and wrong-run snapshot ownership.
- Returned `DELIVERY_SNAPSHOT_NOT_READY` for incomplete `git_auto_delivery` snapshots and unavailable credential/readiness states.
- Required domain failure audit for missing/not-ready delivery snapshot failures and failed closed with `TOOL_AUDIT_REQUIRED_FAILED` when the domain audit service was absent or failed.
- Preserved safe error behavior by redacting unsafe `safe_details` fallback.
- Kept previews free of raw repository/default-branch values; previews expose only configured flags and readiness.
- Verified execution through `ToolRegistry.execute()`, including the `delivery_integration.allowed_tools` gate, audit intent, and run-log recording.
